4. If you don't pay

If you don't pay back the money owed the Department for Work and Pensions may recover the money through a debt collection agency or by taking it directly from your wages.

Recovery from wages

DWP may contact your employer to make deductions directly from your wages under a direct earnings attachment

Recovery through debt collection agency

DWP may pass your case to an independent debt collector if you haven’t paid back the money you owe.

You’ll get a letter from one of the following suppliers saying that it’s acting on behalf of DWP:

  • Akinika (formerly iQor)
  • CCS Collect
  • drydensfairfax solicitors
  • Credit Solutions
  • Fredricksons
  • dlc (Direct Legal & Collections)

You should deal directly with the debt collector to arrange repayment.
